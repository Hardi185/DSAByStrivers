# Problem Statement: 
Given the head of a linked list, rotate the list to the right by k places.

### Examples:

```yaml
Example 1:
Input:
	head = [1,2,3,4,5] 
	k = 2
Output:
 head = [4,5,1,2,3]
Explanation:
 We have to rotate the list to the right twice.
```
